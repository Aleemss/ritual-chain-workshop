# Reflection: Multi-Stage Verification in Bounties

Multi-stage verification adds human oversight through challenges before AI judgment. This creates a robust system where participants can flag suspicious submissions.

**Public vs Hidden:** Commitments are public; answers stay hidden until reveal. Challenges are public and transparent.

**AI vs Human:** AI provides final judgment after human challenges are resolved. This combines human intuition with AI efficiency.

**Advantages:** Multiple verification layers increase trust and fairness.

**Trade-off:** Longer process due to additional phases, but increased security.
